# Skill Ideas

1. **Compliance Matrix Generator** — trigger: "build compliance matrix" →
   takes a piece of legislation or client context and produces a structured
   compliance matrix with obligations, responsible parties, and deadlines

2. **Compliance Report Drafter** — trigger: "draft compliance report" →
   takes notes or a completed matrix and produces a formatted compliance
   report ready for client or board delivery

3. **CRMP Builder** — trigger: "build CRMP" → generates a Compliance Risk
   Management Plan with standard compliance questions tailored to the
   client's sector and regulatory context

4. **Document Formatter** — trigger: "format this document" → takes raw
   or messy content and applies clean, consistent structure — headings,
   bullets, summary table, executive summary — to Lee's Compliance standard

5. **Master Context Loader** — trigger: "load my context" → injects
   Alison's firm background, practice areas, client context, and standard
   matter scope so it never needs to be retyped

6. **Contract Risk Flagger** — trigger: "review this contract" → reads a
   contract and flags legal and commercial risk, ranked by severity, with
   suggested amendments

7. **Gazette Extractor** — trigger: "extract from gazette" → reads a
   Government Gazette or legislation and pulls out structured obligations,
   effective dates, and compliance actions relevant to the client

8. **Weekly Review** — trigger: "weekly review" → prompts Alison through
   a short structured reflection — what moved, what stalled, what's
   priority next week — and saves a summary to outputs/
